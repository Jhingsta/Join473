Help

Welcome to the help page for Join, your guide to using our kanban project management tool. Here, we'll provide an overview of what Join is and how to use it.

What is Join?

Join is a kanban-based project management tool designed and built by a group of dedicated students as part of their web development bootcamp.

Kanban, a Japanese term meaning "billboard", is a highly effective method to visualize work, limit work-in-progress, and maximize efficiency (or flow). Join brings this philosophy to your projects.

It is important to note that Join is designed as an educational exercise and is not intended for extensive business usage. While we strive for a robust and bug-free application, it may not cover all security and scalability requirements for production environments.

How to use it

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use Join:

1. Exploring the Board

When you log in to Join, you'll find a default board. This board represents your project and contains four default lists: "To Do", "In Progress", "Awaiting Feedback", and "Done".

2. Creating Contacts

In Join, you can add contacts to collaborate on your projects. Go to the "Contacts" section, click on "New contact", and fill in the required information.

3. Adding Cards

Now that you've added your contacts, you can start adding cards. Cards represent individual tasks. Click the "+" button under the appropriate list to create a new card and assign it to contacts.

4. Moving Cards

As the status of your tasks changes, you can move the corresponding cards across the lists. Simply drag a card from one list and drop it into another to reflect the task's current stage.
